Services

1. Free Carrier Counselling

We the team of guidance world organize a free counselling to the students so that they can  choose the right path of education according to his talent and knowledge.  We provide a proper guidance to the students so that they can achieve proper college of best education. We also help students to choose streams for their graduation so that they can achieve success in job feild according to their merit.

2. Campus Visit

We make students and their parents a campus visit so that they can know about the campus infrastructure and other facilities of the college.

3. Profiling

Guidance World (GW) aims to transform the lives of students by offering the right guidance for choosing the best platform. To ensure that we will first conduct a comprehensive profiling exercise through our trained professional admission counselors who have in-depth knowledge about our academic partner’s countries, Medical universities and Medical courses. Based on the student’s academic qualifications, career goals, financial status, our counselors will help the students in choosing the right course that matches student’s aspirations and goals.

4. Enrollment Process

Guidance World is associated with renowned and esteemed Medical colleges in countries like Russia, Romania, Belarus, Moldova, Georgia ,Ukraine etc. We have tie-ups with medical Universities/colleges of these countries and it ensures that the students have a hassle-free admission process to the right University of their choice.

5. Country/University/Course Selection

Overseas education is not easy. Overseas admission process involves a lot of do’s/don’ts and it is very costly. Without the guidance of a right counsellor it is impossible to choose a right country to study abroad, right university and right course. Guidance World is the best education counselling service provider that has made the whole overseas admission process easy for the students. The admission counsellor of Guidance World shortlist a number of appropriate overseas colleges based on the academic qualifications, career goals, financial status and work experience of the aspirant. Then the students are given time to discuss the shortlisted colleges or universities with their family members and friends before finalizing the decision.

6. Documentation

Selection in right college and course is not enough – the final step in overseas admission process is the approval of Visa. Guidance World is an education counselling service provider that also assists students in getting visa. It is very important to present each and every document very professionally to the visa issuing authority because a single disorder in document can lead to the rejection or delay of visa. In such case the overseas admission may get cancelled. So once the University and Course are finalized by the students the editorial and review team of Guidance world. reviews each and every document of the students very thoroughly and carefully. In the process of reviewing the necessary documents, the editorial team also improves or corrects the content, grammar, spelling mistakes, tone and structure of the documents. In short, the editorial team presents the document in right manner. They also prepare and groom the students for the interview that they need to face at the specific embassy.

7. Application Review and Submission

After choosing the best overseas university, Guidance World gives the student a final chance to review the application once again before they submit the final application to the chosen university. This is a very important stage and student should highly focus on this stage. As such before the final submission of the application, our highly experienced application professional team sits with the students and ensures that all the documents and details given by the students are correct and accurate.

8. Visa Counselling

It is impossible to get admission in abroad without a relevant visa. Guidance World is the best education counselling service provider that assists students in getting hassle- free visa. The Visa counselling Service of Guidance World makes sure that there is no inadvertent delay or rejection in visa. For this purpose each enrolled student of Guidance World is assigned with a Study Abroad Visa Consultant who guides the students in every step of visa approval process, starting from the collation of proper document required in the visa application to all the key steps of the interview that students needs to face at the High Commission or Embassy.

9. Cloud counselling

Guidance World not only provides face to face education counselling but also offers cloud counselling or distance counselling. The highly trained professional counsellor of Guidance World energize the mindset of student through cloud counselling that helps a student in achieving his/her goal.

10. Travel Assistance

We all know that overseas education is very costly. At Guidance World, we always try to lessen the financial burden of the students and parents. That is the reason why we provide travel assistance to all our enrolled students. We have tie-ups with many domestic and international travelling agents. Thus we can make the cost of air ticket, foreign exchange and travel insurance at affordable prices. Apart from that we line up three competitive bids for each service from our vendors.

11. Post-Arrival Services

Guidance World offers something beyond counselling. It also offers post arrival services to its students. We know that the presence of a companion or acquaintance in a foreign land is invaluable; therefore, Guidance World provides the following offers to their enrolled students who have taken admission in foreign universities:
Pickup facility are provided from the airport.
Fully-furnished boarding room are provided with the facility of Wi-Fi.
At the time of travelling breakfast, lunch, dinner and refreshment are provided.
Facilities of picking and dropping the students to their colleges and universities are provided.
Assistance is also provided in finding and leasing apartment, vehicles, etc.
Overview of complete public transport facilities in the foreign land is also provided.

12. Education Bank Loan Assistance

Guidance World will facilitate loan from banks. It has a separate loan assistance cell that helps students to get educational loan at the time of overseas admission.
